Overview

Audience

The audience to this project are both its members and visitors to the area. Members would benefit from resources and networking opportunities offered by the chamber, while visitors could benefit from information about local businesses and events. By targeting both audiences, the chamber can attract new members and support the local business community while also promoting the area to visitors.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1:

    A guild member needs advice on how to grow their business and improve their finances.

  • Scenario 2:

    The guild organizes a charity event to raise funds for a local charitable organization.

  • Scenario 3:

    Visitor seeking information on educational and training programs in business administration.

  • Scenario 4:

    Person interested in learning about the opportunities and benefits of joining the guild of business administrators.

  • Scenario 5:

    Visitor interested in attending networking events to establish business contacts.
