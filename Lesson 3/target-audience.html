Overview

Audience

The audience to this project are both its members and visitors to the area. Members would benefit from resources and networking opportunities offered by the chamber, while visitors could benefit from information about local businesses and events. By targeting both audiences, the chamber can attract new members and support the local business community while also promoting the area to visitors.
