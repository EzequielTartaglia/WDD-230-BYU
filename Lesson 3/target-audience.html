Content

This site will have the mission of being able to provide the technological ecosystem with enough information to understand the skills and competencies of Ezequiel M. Tartaglia as a software engineer.

The doubts that people who visit the cycle may have and that will be answered through this site will be whether the candidate is suitable to be able to participate in the goals and projects of the company (the stacks that the individual has), addressing studies and experiences . practices that he has had, in addition to his professional experience.

What can be done through this site is to have the summary of the individual, as well as a brief introduction and the opportunity to contact him directly.

The information necessary to achieve the objective in this case is through links and pdfs to be able to provide the necessary information.
